⇄ Conexus-specific decisions vs Ascend baseline
  • Palette: Conexus navy #1B2A4E + gold #FAAA1C (Ascend baseline uses Charcoal #1C1C1C + Ascend Blue #45AADD)
  • Scope: AML-focused board — copy and category structure emphasize advanced manufacturing and logistics. Ascend's platform spans all industries.
  • Typography: Poppins body / Archivo Black display (Ascend uses Archivo per brand guide). Confirm Conexus brand font with their team.
  • Co-branding: "Powered by The Ascend Network" credited in the footer and sign-up hero — Ascend pages don't carry this.
